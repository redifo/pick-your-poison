1. After clicking Play the Game button you will asked for a name input.
  2. Enter your name and press Submit to start the game.
  3. On the top left of the screen you can see your score.
  4. On the top right of the screen you can see a timer, that records the total time spent answering a question.
  5. Once an answer is selected, the selected answer will turn green if correct and red if wrong.
  6. Once a question is answered, the timer stops and a next button appears.
  7. Clicking the next button shows the next question and the timer continues.
  8. Answer all 10 questions to complete the quiz and see your results.
